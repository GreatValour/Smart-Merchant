Marketing Intern

Join our team

Overview

Our college internship program offers students valuable work experience, professional development, and mentorship over the summer and during the academic year. Our intern position provides fantastic marketing experience. Students are exposed to workplace discipline and supervised by subject matter experts, who assist them in developing the skills, knowledge, and experience essential to succeed in today's technically demanding jobs. Interns at Smart Merchant Processing work across many divisions.

Our internships are project-based, thus the skills we seek vary depending on the needs of the projects during each term. This is a wonderful opportunity to advance your career. We encourage all majors to apply. Internships are part-time (15 hours per week) during the academic year and full-time (40 hours per week) over the summer.

Responsibilities

  • Evangelizing our brand strategy and developing distinctive assets to creatively and articulately position our identity.
  • Marketing the entirety of our unrivaled array of software, goods, and services, stressing our competitive advantage.
  • Creating campaigns to raise awareness, change preferences, and increase customer engagement and demand.
  • Working on genuine marketing activities to help the company.
  • Participating in various marketing activities, such as digital marketing, event organizing, branding, and market research.
  • Collaborating with several departments to better understand and meet their marketing needs.
  • Contributing to the planning and implementation of marketing strategies.
  • Assisting with the creation of marketing materials such as brochures, presentations, and promotional content.
  • Participating in weekly training sessions to improve understanding of comprehensive marketing techniques.
  • Assisting the Digital Marketing Manager with digital marketing activities such as SEO, Paid Search, Emails, Social Media, Performance Analytics, and Reporting.

This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time with or without notice.
