Grocery & Pharmacy

Restaurants

We will make available the necessary tools your restaurant will need to accept any type of payments used regularly including credit card acceptance, PIN debit, and telecheck solutions.

Retailers

Whether you are a national or local retailer you efforts tend to be concentrated on marketing and customer retention and because of this, SMP will outfit you with the best software and technology needed to help you receive payments.

Car Dealerships

SMP will outfit your institution with terminal platforms that will allow you to receive credit and debit card transactions.

Municipalities

Healthcare

Hospitality

E-Commerce

The need to provide customers with the option to pay for products and or services online has gone from an option to a necessity for many businesses. Our knowledge of the ever changing technology will allow your business to maximize its online presence.

Professional Services
